My favourite dish: ICE CREAMS

A white van with drawings of chocolate, strawberry and vanilla ice cream moves through the street. All the children in the neighbourhood follow the little ding of its bell and immediately get into a line. The ice cream van has come with the ice cream man. Some children have already decided what they want to eat, others want to try a new flavour. The ice cream man opens the shutter and smiles warmly at the kids. They all look up at him eagerly.

One by one he collects their orders and the children look up at the short, tubby man as he places scoops into cones.
